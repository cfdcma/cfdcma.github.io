2015 Season Trips

In 2015, the Epic Pass partakers explored all of its Colorado offerings over the course of two trips. Mid-March skiing in Breckenridge served up fresh snow day after day. With all of the ski resorts on the Epic Pass in easy driving distance, you can try them all out during a week in Colorado and have time to revisit your favorite.

Kirkwood

A little off the beaten path in the Sierra Mountains, Kirkwood is the perfect mountain respite from the daily grind. Rent a condo or book a hotel room for a long weekend and enjoy some of the most challenging skiing in the Tahoe area. With a level 5 avalanche rating guarantee steep slopes so make sure your edges are freshly sharpened and your boards and skis are waxed! With more limited crowds than other Tahoe area ski resorts, you can walk your front door and be on a lift in minutes to maximize your runs. Cook in or make the drive to a local restaurant and casinos after your day on the slopes.

Arapahoe Basin

Arapahoe Basin (A-Basin) was a repeat visit from the 2014 season and a favorite. A first stop at A-Basin from sea level locations can present altitude acclimation challenges so pacing yourself and resting as needed is recommended. This year the back bowls had the best conditions to practice your skills and take in the view. After a full day on the mountain, the 6th Alley Bar and Grill at the base serves up cold refreshments and food when you need to refuel before your next stop.
